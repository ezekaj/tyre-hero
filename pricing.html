Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in complete transparency. The prices listed include the cost of the tyre, fitting, balancing, and disposal of your old tyre. There are no additional callout fees, even for emergency services.

What if you don't have my tyre size in stock?

Our mobile units carry a wide range of common tyre sizes. If we don't have your specific size in stock, we can usually source it within a few hours. In emergency situations, we may be able to fit a temporary tyre to get you moving safely until we can return with your exact size.

Do you charge extra for weekend or evening services?

No, our pricing remains the same regardless of the day or time. We're available 24/7, including weekends and bank holidays, at no extra charge.

What's the difference between budget, mid-range, and premium tyres?

Budget tyres are cost-effective options suitable for everyday driving. Mid-range tyres offer a balance of performance and value. Premium tyres from top brands like Michelin, Continental, and Bridgestone provide superior performance, handling, and longevity. Our technicians can help you choose the right option based on your vehicle, driving habits, and budget.

Can you repair any puncture?

Not all punctures can be safely repaired. We follow British Standard BSAU159 guidelines, which specify that punctures can only be repaired if they're in the central three-quarters of the tread area and are no larger than 6mm in diameter. Punctures in the sidewall, shoulder area, or those that are too large cannot be safely repaired and will require tyre replacement.

Do you offer any discounts for multiple tyres?

Yes, we offer discounts when you replace multiple tyres at the same time. For two tyres, you'll receive a 5% discount on the total cost. For three or more tyres, the discount increases to 10%. This applies to both scheduled and emergency services.
