Our Story

How TyreHero went from a simple idea to the UK's leading mobile tyre service.

From Frustration to Innovation

TyreHero was born out of frustration. In 2020, our founder James Mitchell found himself stranded on the M25 with a flat tyre late at night. After waiting over three hours for roadside assistance, he knew there had to be a better way.

James, a former automotive engineer, teamed up with logistics expert Sarah Chen to create a mobile tyre service that prioritized speed, convenience, and customer satisfaction. They started with just two vans serving the London area.

Today, TyreHero operates a fleet of over 100 mobile units across the UK, with technicians available 24/7 to help drivers in need. We've helped over 50,000 customers get back on the road safely, and we're just getting started.

Our commitment to innovation continues to drive us forward. We're constantly improving our technology, expanding our coverage, and finding new ways to make the tyre replacement experience as seamless as possible.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us and shapes everything we do.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the tyre industry by providing fast, convenient, and professional mobile tyre services that prioritize customer safety and satisfaction.

We aim to be the first choice for drivers across the UK when they need tyre services, whether it's an emergency situation or a scheduled replacement.

By bringing the garage to our customers, we save them time, reduce stress, and ensure they can get back on the road safely and quickly.

Our Values

  • Safety First

  • Reliability

  • Transparency
