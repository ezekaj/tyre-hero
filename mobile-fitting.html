Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our mobile tyre fitting service

How quickly can you get to my location?

+

For emergency services, our average response time is 90 minutes. For scheduled appointments, we'll arrive within your chosen time window. We always call 30 minutes before arrival to confirm and provide an accurate ETA.

What areas do you cover for mobile fitting?

+

We provide mobile tyre fitting services in Slough, Maidenhead & Windsor, covering all major cities, towns, and motorway networks. Our extensive network ensures rapid response times nationwide. Check our coverage page for specific areas.

Do you have the right tyres for my vehicle?

+

We stock a comprehensive range of tyres for cars, vans, 4x4s, and commercial vehicles from leading brands. If we don't have your specific tyre in our mobile inventory, we can source it quickly and reschedule at no extra cost.

Is mobile fitting as good as garage service?

+

Yes! Our mobile service uses the same professional-grade equipment found in premium tyre centers. Our technicians are RAC certified and follow the same industry standards for safety and quality.

What if the weather is bad?

+

We work in most weather conditions and carry weather protection equipment. Only extreme conditions (heavy snow, ice, or dangerous storms) may require rescheduling for safety reasons.

Do you offer wheel balancing?

+

Yes, our premium service includes professional wheel balancing using portable balancing equipment. This ensures smooth driving and prevents premature tyre wear.

Are there any callout fees or hidden charges?

+

No callout fees ever! Our pricing is completely transparent with no hidden charges, mileage fees, or weekend surcharges. The price quoted includes everything except the tyres themselves.

Can I watch the fitting process?

+

Absolutely! We encourage customers to observe the process if they wish. Our technicians are happy to explain what they're doing and answer any questions about your tyres or vehicle.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, contactless payments, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. Payment is only due after successful completion of the service to your satisfaction.

What warranty do you provide?

+

All our workmanship comes with a 12-month warranty. Tyres carry their manufacturer's warranty. If there are any issues with our fitting, we'll return to fix them at no charge.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

+

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel with at least 2 hours notice for free. Contact us on 0800 123 4567 or use our online booking system. We understand plans change and try to be flexible.

Do you service commercial and fleet vehicles?

+

Yes! We offer specialized commercial and fleet services with volume discounts, scheduled maintenance programs, and dedicated account management. Contact us for a custom quote.
