Can Your Puncture Be Safely Repaired?

Our expert technicians follow strict industry standards to determine repairability

Usually Repairable

85% Success Rate
  • Small nail or screw punctures (up to 6mm)
  • Clean entry wounds in tread area
  • Single punctures with good tyre condition
  • Tread depth above 2mm legal minimum
  • No sidewall or shoulder damage
  • Straight-through punctures without tearing

Replacement Required

Safety First
  • Sidewall punctures or cuts
  • Multiple punctures in same area
  • Tears larger than 6mm diameter
  • Tyre worn below legal limit (1.6mm)
  • Age-related cracking or degradation
  • Previous repairs in same area
  • Run-flat tyres (manufacturer dependent)

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about puncture repair

How long does a professional puncture repair last?

When performed to BS AU 159f standards with quality materials, a professional puncture repair should last the lifetime of the tyre. We provide a 2-year warranty, but most repairs last much longer. The key is proper assessment - we only repair punctures that can be safely fixed.

What size puncture can you repair?

We can typically repair punctures up to 6mm in diameter located in the tread area. The puncture must be straight-through without tearing. Smaller punctures (nail holes, small screws) have the highest success rate. Our technician will assess each case individually.

Why can't sidewall punctures be repaired?

The sidewall flexes constantly during driving and experiences the highest stress. Repairs in this area cannot achieve the same strength as the original construction and may fail unexpectedly. For safety, industry standards prohibit sidewall repairs - replacement is the only safe option.

How much can I save with a repair vs replacement?

Repairs cost ¬£25-¬£35 compared to ¬£60-¬£150+ for replacement (depending on tyre type). You could save ¬£35-¬£125 when repair is possible. However, we never compromise safety for savings - if replacement is needed, we'll provide honest advice and competitive prices.

Can you repair punctures on run-flat tyres?

This depends on the manufacturer and whether the tyre was driven on while flat. Some run-flat tyres can be repaired if caught early and not driven on extensively. Our technician will assess the tyre condition and consult manufacturer guidelines before proceeding.

Do you provide emergency puncture repair?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency puncture repair service with a 90-minute response time. Emergency repairs cost ¬£35 and include the same professional assessment and repair process. We can perform repairs roadside if safe, or arrange recovery if needed.

What happens if my puncture can't be repaired?

If we determine the puncture cannot be safely repaired, we'll explain why and provide a quote for replacement. There's no charge for the assessment if you choose our replacement service. We'll never proceed with an unsafe repair - your safety is our priority.

Is wheel balancing included with repairs?

Yes, professional wheel balancing is included with every repair at no extra cost. Since we remove the tyre from the rim for proper repair, we always rebalance before refitting to ensure smooth driving and even tyre wear.
