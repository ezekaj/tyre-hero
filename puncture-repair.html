Our Professional Repair Process

Industry-standard procedures for safe, lasting repairs

1

Initial Assessment

Thorough inspection to determine if the puncture can be safely repaired according to industry standards.

2

Tyre Removal

Safe removal of the tyre from the rim to access the puncture from inside the tyre.

3

Professional Repair

Application of industry-standard patch and plug repair using professional-grade materials.

4

Quality Testing

Inflation test and final inspection to ensure the repair meets safety standards and will last.

5

Remounting & Balancing

Professional remounting on the rim with proper balancing to ensure smooth driving.

6

Final Check

Comprehensive safety check and pressure adjustment before you drive away safely.
