Our Hiring Process

Professional, transparent, and efficient recruitment process designed to find the best fit for both you and TyreHero

1

Application Submission

Submit your application through our website or email. Include your CV, cover letter, and any relevant qualifications or certifications.

Timeline: Immediate
What to include: CV, cover letter, qualifications
Tip: Tailor your application to the specific role
2

Initial Screening

Our recruitment team reviews applications against role criteria. Successful candidates receive a phone call to discuss experience and availability.

Timeline: 24-48 hours
Duration: 15-20 minute phone call
Focus: Experience match and initial fit assessment
3

Competency Interview

Detailed interview (video call or in-person) covering technical skills, experience, and cultural fit. For technical roles, may include practical assessment.

Timeline: Within 1 week
Duration: 45-60 minutes
Format: Competency-based questions + practical elements
4

Final Interview & Assessment

Meet with the hiring manager and potential team members. For senior roles, may include presentation or case study discussion.

Timeline: Within 5 days of initial interview
Duration: 30-45 minutes
Attendees: Hiring manager + team representatives
5

Reference & Background Checks

We conduct professional reference checks and necessary background verification including DBS checks for relevant positions.

Timeline: 2-3 days
Requirements: 2 professional references
Checks: Employment history, DBS (where required)
6

Job Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates receive a formal job offer with contract details, followed by comprehensive onboarding and training program.

Timeline: 1-2 days after references
Includes: Contract, benefits package, start date
Next: 6-week onboarding program begins

Interview Preparation

We provide guidance on what to expect and offer reasonable adjustments for accessibility needs. Contact our HR team if you need support.

Feedback Promise

All candidates receive constructive feedback regardless of outcome. We believe in helping everyone improve their interview skills.

Equal Opportunities

Our hiring process is designed to be fair, inclusive, and free from discrimination. We welcome applications from all backgrounds.

We offer genuine career progression, competitive packages, and a supportive culture where your contributions are recognized and rewarded. We operate 24/7 emergency services, so offer flexible working patterns including standard day shifts (8am-6pm), evening shifts (2pm-10pm), and night shifts (10pm-6am). Many positions offer flexible scheduling, and we always try to accommodate personal commitments like family obligations.

We operate across the entire UK and are continuously expanding. Current major hubs include London, Birmingham, Manchester, Leeds, Glasgow, and Cardiff. We regularly open new positions in growing markets, so even if we don't currently recruit in your area, we may do so soon.

Mobile technicians receive fully equipped company vans with all necessary tools, equipment, and technology. Fuel cards cover all business travel. Management roles include car allowances or company cars. All equipment is regularly maintained and updated to ensure you have the best tools for the job.

All employees start with 25 days annual leave plus UK bank holidays. This increases with length of service, reaching 30 days after 5 years. We also offer birthday leave (an extra day off each year), study leave for professional development, and flexible arrangements for important family events.

All new hires complete a comprehensive 6-week onboarding program covering health & safety, technical skills, customer service excellence, and company culture.
