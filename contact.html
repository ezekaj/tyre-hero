Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us.

What's the best way to contact you in an emergency?

For emergency tyre services, the fastest way to reach us is by calling our 24/7 emergency line at 0800 123 4567. Our operators are available around the clock and can dispatch a technician to your location immediately.

How quickly will you respond to my email inquiry?

We aim to respond to all email inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer support line at 0800 123 4568 for faster assistance.

Can I visit your location to get my tyres fitted?

TyreHero is a mobile tyre fitting service, which means we come to you rather than you coming to us. Our headquarters is an administrative office and doesn't offer tyre fitting services on-site. We're happy to send a mobile technician to your preferred location instead.

How do I provide feedback about your service?

We value your feedback! You can share your experience by filling out the contact form on this page, emailing us at feedback@tyrehero.com, or calling our customer support line. After each service, you'll also receive an email with a link to our customer satisfaction survey.

TyreHero provides 24/7 emergency mobile tyre fitting and replacement services across the UK. Our professional technicians come to you, whether you're at home, work, or stranded roadside.
