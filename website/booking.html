Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our mobile tyre service

How quickly can you get to me?

+

Our average response time is 90 minutes for emergency services and within your chosen time slot for scheduled appointments. We'll always call 30 minutes before arrival to confirm.

What areas do you cover?

+

We provide 24/7 mobile tyre services across the UK, including major cities and motorway coverage. Check our locations page for specific coverage in your area.

Do you carry tyres for all vehicle types?

+

Yes, we carry a wide range of premium and budget tyres for cars, vans, 4x4s, and commercial vehicles. If we don't have your specific tyre in stock, we can source it quickly.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, contactless payments, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. Payment is due after successful completion of the service.

Is there a callout fee?

+

No callout fees ever! Our transparent pricing includes all labor and travel costs. You only pay for the tyres and fitting service - no hidden charges.

Can I book for someone else?

+

Absolutely! You can book our service for family members, friends, or colleagues. Just ensure the person receiving the service is aware of the appointment and can be present.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

+

No problem! Just give us at least 2 hours notice for free cancellation or rescheduling. You can call us on 0800 123 4567 or use our online booking system.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

+

Yes! All our work comes with a 12-month warranty covering both parts and labor. We also offer manufacturers' warranties on all tyres supplied.
